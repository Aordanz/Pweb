Champions Tour 2024

The biggest event VALORANT Champions Tour 2024 will soon be held in Seoul, South Korea! On August 1–25, 8 of the best teams in the world will compete for the Champions trophy and the title of VALORANT World Champion! Champions is the final event of the VALORANT Champions Tour and is the culmination of the VALORANT competitive season. During this year, top teams from all over the world compete in their respective International Leagues to qualify by becoming the top of the standings or collecting the highest Championship Points throughout the season.

Welcome to the top of the VALORANT Champions Tour 2024! The stage is ready, the lights are on, and everyone is waiting for the start of the biggest tournament of the year! After a year of intense competition and ups and downs, the best VALORANT teams from all over the world arrived at Champions Seoul! On 1–25 August, competitors will compete through the Group Stage to advance to the double elimination Playoffs to the Grand Final which will determine the new World Champion! Group Stage (1–11 August) The Group Stage was attended by 8 teams spread across four groups, and each group had one representative from the four VALORANT International Leagues. At this stage, the participating teams will compete in a double elimination format, and the two strongest teams will advance from each group. Playoff Stage (14–25 August) The eight qualifying teams will be randomly assigned to a double elimination bracket, and teams from the same group will be assigned to the opposite bracket. These teams will compete in a Best-of-3 format, until the last two matches will use a Best-of-5 format. The team that survives until the end will win the title of VALORANT World Champion!
